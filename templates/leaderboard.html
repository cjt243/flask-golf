How It Works

The Game

Pick 5 golfers across 3 tiers each week. $5 entry. Winner takes the pot.

Tiers

  • Tier 1 — Top 5 players · pick 1
  • Tier 2 — Players 6–16 · pick 2
  • Tier 3 — Players 17+ · pick 2

Ranked by DraftKings salary each week.

Scoring

Your team score is the sum of your 5 golfers' tournament scores. Lowest total wins.

The Cut

  • Made cut — Actual score used (capped at the cut line)
  • Missed cut — Score becomes cut line + 1 stroke

Prizes

100% of the weekly pot goes to the winner.
